Sewing is for everyone, from those first learning how to thread a needle to professional designer sewists. If you want to learn how to sew you just have to give it a try.Just remember practics makes a man perfect.

Sewing for Beginners

"Have you always wanted to learn to sew? For beginner to get you started you will need sewing clothing, home goods,niddles and threads. we will help you to learn about different sewing techniques, sewing tips, and sewing tricks to sew and to sew well. With our sewing tutorials you’ll learn how to sew clothes and basic sewing stitches. These sewing basics are really geared towards apparel sewing, but lots of these techniques are used in all sewing crafts and practices. You’ll also learn about zippers, hemming, finishes seams, and much more!"

Making Your Own Clothes

Nobody wants to spend a fortune on new clothes. With the help of our free clothing patterns and ideas, now you don’t have to! Eliminate the stress of staying in style when you learn to sew clothes with these stunning tutorials.

Home Dec Sewing

Learn how to sew throw pillows and make DIY curtains using these free ideas for home decor crafts. These free sewing patterns will show you how to do home decor on a budget.

Hand Embroidery

Love working with your hands? Give embroidery a try! In this comprehensive beginner’s class, we will walk you through the basics from choosing fabrics and floss to hooping up and crafting beautiful stitches. Learn to create a gorgeous sampler as you master a range of key techniques, including how to work with letters, numbers and finished garments, finish flat embroidery and more.
